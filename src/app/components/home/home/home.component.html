What We Do

Joy Children's Home Ruiru is a place dedicated to take care of homeless children, and other children that may require our help. Our main goal is to create an environment where the children will feel like home and have a chance to create a great future for themselves because we believe they are the leaders of tomorrow.

We do our best to make sure our children meet the right people who may help them build up on their interests. These interests may be educational or even talents. Pushing our children to work hard on what they like to do helps us enhance their sense of self awareness.
